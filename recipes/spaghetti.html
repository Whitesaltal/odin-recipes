Spaghetti

Description

An Italian dish consisting largely of spaghetti, typically with a sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt, ground black pepper, add to taste
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 TBS dried parsley flakes
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Crushed red pepper flakes, add to taste
  • 1 TBS worcestershire sauce
  • 1 TBS granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • spaghetti noodles of your choice

Instructions

  1. Boil water

    2. start to boil water for pasta then begin working on the sauce

  2. Season Beef

    3. Add salt and pepper to your liking mix thoroughly

  3. Beef

    4. Add beef to a large skillet medium to high heat. Mix in the chopped onion

    Drain excess grease leaving about 1 TBS in skillet

  4. Mix in sauce

    5. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, italian seasoning, parsley, garlic powder, crushed red pepper, worcestershire, and sugar to skillet

    Mix thoroughly

    Reduce heat and let simmer for 30 minutes, occasionally stirring

    Add chopped basil

  5. Back to pasta

    6. While sauce is cooking Water should be boiled by this point

    Add pasta and cook until al dante following pasta directions on box

    Stir often to prevent sticking
