Lasagna

Description

a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 medium onion,diced (3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, yellow), diced (3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 28oz can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3oz tomato paste
  • 14oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 TBS oregano
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1 tbs italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder/salt
  • 1 TBS red or white vinegar
  • 1 TBS to 1/4 cup sugar add to your desired taste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pound mozzarella, grates or sliced
  • 1/4 pound grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta

    bring a large pot of salt water to a boil. While water is boiling prepare sauce

  2. Brown Ground Beef

    In a large skillet medium heat ad 2 tbs olive oil, add your meat, cook until lightly browned

    Place meat into a bowl and drain all fat except a TBS

  3. Cook the bell peppers, onions, garlic, add beef

    Add your diced bell peppers and onions into the skillet

    Cook for 4-5 minutes, until onions are translucent and peppers are soft

    Mix in your minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds

    Add ground beef back into skillet

    Mix everything together and cook for 5 minutes

  4. Building The Sauce

    Transfer cooked mixture into a medium to large pot

    Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into pot

    Mix in parsley, oregano, italian seasoning, garlic powder/salt, add more for desired taste

    Bring sauce ot a simmer then lower heat. Low simmer for 15-45 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure nothing sticks and flavors mix

    Remove from heat

  5. Back to the noodles

    Water should be boiled by this point

    Add pasta and cook until al dante following pasta directions on box

    Stir often to prevent sticking

    When ready drain and rinse in cold water

    On a baking sheet or plate spread light amounts of olive oil over each noodle. You can spread them out over a baking sheet rubbing both sides or do one by one on a plate.

    Heat Oven to 375Â°F

  6. Assemble the lasagna

    In a 9x13-inch casserole or lasagna dish, ladle a cup of sauce and spread it over the bottom of the dish. Arrange one layer of lasagna noodles lengthwise (about 3 long noodles, edge may overlap, depending on your pan) over the sauce. Ladle a third of the remaining sauce over the noodles.

    Sprinkle a layer of a third of the grated mozzarella on top of lasagna sauce. Add half of the ricotta cheese, by placing cheese dollops every couple inches. Sprinkle half the grated parmesan cheese evenly over the top of ricotta cheese.

    Apply second layer of noodles, top it with half of the remaining sauce. Add half of the remaining Mozzarella, the remaining ricotta cheese, and another the remaining Parmesan.

    Finish with another layer of noodles. Spread the remaining sauce over the top layer of noodles and sprinkle with the remaining Mozzarella cheese.

  7. Bake

    Cover pan with aluminum foil, tented slightly so it doesn't touch noodles.

    Bake at 375 for 45 minutes

    Remove aluminum foil for the last 10 minutes if you would like a crusted top or edges

  8. Cool and serve

    Remove lasagna from oven and let cool for about 15 minutes

    Cut and serve
